## What is BlazeNeuro?

BlazeNeuro is a modern blog and community platform featuring:

- **Real-time Chat**: Discord-style community chat with channels and DMs
- **Blog System**: Markdown-powered blog with rich editor
- **Admin Dashboard**: Content management for admins
- **Role-based Access**: User, Admin, and SuperAdmin roles
- **Flow Scripts**: Visual workflow automation
